Wecome to Interwire Communications Solutions. You will find lots of useful information in this handbook. When you arrive, go to reception. They will make you an identification badge. You will show this at security. Next, report to your supervisor's cubicle. He or she will direct you to the appropriate aisle. This is where you will find your workstation. All workstations are arranged in an open plan. Take note of the fire exits closest to your workstation. Your supervisor will direct you to the conference room. 1. break room - a place where employees can socialize or eat
2. common area - intended for general employee usage
3. conference room - a large room where meetings take place
4. reception - a place where new visitors to a building stop first
5. security - the department responsible for the safety of a building
6. workstation - a place with an employee's desk and computer

1. calculator - a device used to perform mathematical calculations.
2. computer - a device for storing and processing data.
3. display monitor - a large overhead screen.
4. keyboard - keys used to enter data into a computer.
5. mouse - a handheld device used to operate a computer.
6. phone - a device used to make and receive telephone calls.

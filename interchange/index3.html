01. That's what friends are for!

C: Do you have a date for your friend's wedding yet?
K: Actually, no, I don't... Do you know anyone I could go with?
C: Hmm. What kind of guys do you like?
K: Oh, I like guys who aren't too serious and who have a good sense of humor. You know, someone like you.
C: OK. Uh, waht else?
K: Well, I'd prefer someone I have somthing in common with - whi I can talk to easily.
C: I think I know just the guy for you. Bob Branson. Do you know him?
K: No, I don't think so.
C: OK, I'll ask him to meet us for coffee, and you can tell me what you think.
---
C: So, what's the verdict? What did you think of Bob?
K: Well, I was worried at first - especially when I saw that he rode a huge motorcycle. I thought he might be one of those guys who is into heavy metal music and stuff like that. You know what I mean?
C: But he's just a regular kind of guy, right?
K: Yeah, we got along really well!
C: I knew you'd like him.
K: Yeah, I do. And he's really funny. He had me laughing so hard at the coffee shop, remember? I think people sitting next to us thought we were crazy.
C: So are you two going to get together again?
K: Definitely. In fact, we're going to the wedding together!
C: That's great!

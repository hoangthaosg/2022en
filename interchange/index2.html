01. A time to remember

T: Oh, I'm really sorry. Are you OK?
A: I'm fine. But I'm not very good at this.
T: Neither am I... Hey, I like your shirt. Are you from Argentina?
A: Yes, I am, originally. I was born there.
T: Did you grow up there?
A: Yes, I did, but my family moved here 10 years ago, when I was in middle school.
T: And where did you learn to skate?
A: Here in the park. This is only my third time.
T: Well, it's my first time. Can you give me some lessons?
A: Sure. Just follow me.
T: By the way, my name is Ted.
A: And I'm Ana. Nice to meet you.
---
T: Hey, that was fun. Thanks for the lesson!
A: No problem. So, tell me a little about yourself. What do you do?
T: I work in a travel agency.
A: Really! What do you do there?
T: I'm in charge of their computers.
A: Oh. so you're a computer specialist.
T: Well, sort of. Yeah, I guess so.
A: That's great. Then maybe you can give me some help with a computer course I'm taking.
T: Oh, sure... but only if you promise to give me some more skating lessons.
A: It's a deal!
